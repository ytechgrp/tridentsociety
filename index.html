- Trident Enrichment Society -

The Trident Enrichment Society is a registered non-profit, community service organization based in the BC Lower Mainland. Solely operated by volunteers, Trident organizes a wide variety of activities throughout the year in support of its efforts in promoting fundraising, education and good fellowship. The membership is made up of mostly young Asian professionals who have recently graduated from university or college and who are early in their careers.
